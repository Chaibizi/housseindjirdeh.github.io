About Me

A few years later, I landed my first internship at a small software start-up developing an Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) for manufacturing companies.

In my last year of university, I had an idea. With two other university students, I pitched to a startup incubator at University of Toronto, the Hatchery, and got accepted. So where am I right now? I currently work at Deloitte Digital, a full-service digital firm, where I help tackle all sorts of digital challenges big and small. In my current project, I'm developing a single-page application for Sonnet Insurance, a major P&C insurance company, using Rails to build a JSON REST API that interacts with an AngularJS front-end.
